The Story

When Esther Bailey boards the RMS Titanic as its first female officer, her welcome is lukewarm—and no one is more unwelcoming than her own mentor, First Officer Murdoch.

His cool dismissal of her only turns colder once they set sail, with Esther’s short temper challenging his own. But as the line between duty and attraction begins to blur, she and Murdoch find themselves questioning what matters most: their careers . . . or the feelings they’re hiding from each other.

As tragedy unfolds around them, will they overcome the sinking together and live to see daylight?

Blending fact with fiction, On the Edge of Daylight is a slow burn, heart-wrenching historical romance that tells a different story of the RMS Titanic through the eyes of the crew, and shows the duty, loyalty, and sacrifice of two officers who were never supposed to fall for each other.
